Certificate Verification

Enter the certificate code to instantly confirm its authenticity.

Tip: Codes start with UO- and are not case-sensitive.
1
Find the unique code on your certificate document. It starts with UO- followed by the year and a number.
2
Type or paste it into the field above and press Verify or hit Enter.
3
Your certificate details and validity status will appear instantly. You can also view the certificate image.

Certificate not found?

Double-check for typos. Codes are formatted as UO-YYYY-NNN. Contact us if you believe there is an error.

Need official confirmation?

Email us at ushaqlarimizaoyredek@gmail.com for written confirmation.
