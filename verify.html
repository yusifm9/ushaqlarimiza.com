Authenticity Check

Certificate Verification

Instantly verify whether a certificate issued by Ushaqlarimiza Oyredek is authentic and valid.

How It Works

Trustworthy and Transparent

All certificates issued by our organisation carry a unique code. Anyone can use this page to instantly confirm a certificate's authenticity with no login required.

1

Locate the Code

Find the unique certificate code printed on your certificate document. Codes start with UO- followed by the year and a number.

2

Enter and Verify

Type or paste the code into the field and click the Verify button, or press Enter.

3

View the Result

The full certificate details, including recipient name, type, date, and validity status, will be displayed instantly.

All certificate data is verified against our official registry. This process is secure and private.

Verify a Certificate

Enter the certificate code exactly as it appears on the document, for example: UO-2024-001

Tip: Certificate codes always start with UO- and are not case-sensitive.

Certificate not found?

Double-check the code for typos. Codes are formatted as UO-YYYY-NNN. Contact us if you believe there is an error.

Need official confirmation?

For written confirmation email us at info@ushaqlarimizaoyredek.com.

Suspect a fraudulent certificate?

If you believe a fraudulent certificate is in circulation, please report it to our team immediately. We take integrity very seriously.
