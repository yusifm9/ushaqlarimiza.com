Certificate Verification

Instantly verify whether a certificate issued by Ushaqlarimiza Öyrədək is authentic and valid.

How It Works

Trustworthy & Transparent

All certificates issued by our organisation carry a unique code. Anyone — employers, schools, or individuals — can use this page to instantly confirm a certificate's authenticity with no login required.

1

Locate the Code

Find the unique certificate code printed on the bottom or back of your certificate document.

2

Enter & Verify

Type the code into the verification field and click the "Verify" button.

3

View the Result

See the full certificate details — recipient name, type, date, and current validity status.

All certificate data is verified against our official registry. This process is secure and private.

🎓 Verify a Certificate

Enter the certificate code exactly as it appears on the document (e.g. UO-2024-001).

Tip: Certificate codes always start with UO- followed by the year and a number. They are not case-sensitive.

Suspect a fake?
