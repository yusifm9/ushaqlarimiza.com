Our Story

A Student-Led Movement for Every Child

Founded in June 2023, "Ushaqlarimiza Oyredek" is a non-governmental, non-profit and student-led organisation committed to the advancement of disadvantaged, disabled, and underprivileged children across the entire world.

Our team has grown to over 100 members across various divisions. We have extended our reach beyond Azerbaijan, with initiatives now running in the PRC, Hong Kong SAR, and the UK.
