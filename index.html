Our Goals

What We Stand For

Five principles guide everything we do, from our events to the way we treat every child and family we work with.

Diversity

We celebrate every background, culture, and story. Diversity is our greatest strength.

Inclusivity

No child is excluded. Our doors, programmes, and hearts are open to all children regardless of circumstance.

Love

Every interaction is rooted in genuine care, empathy, and warmth for the children we serve.

Happiness

We believe joy is a right, not a privilege. We bring smiles to every event and workshop we run.

Moments

We create memories that children carry for life, precious, meaningful moments that shape who they become.

Our Story

A Student-Led Movement for Every Child

Founded in June 2023, "Ushaqlarimiza Oyredek" is a non-governmental, non-profit and student-led organisation committed to the advancement of disadvantaged, disabled, and underprivileged children across the entire world.

Our primary objective is to offer equal opportunities to children of all backgrounds, ensuring their optimal development at every stage. Since its inception, our team has grown to encompass over 100 members across various divisions.

Additionally, "Ushaqlarimiza Oyredek" has extended its reach beyond the borders of Azerbaijan, undertaking similar initiatives in other countries such as PRC, Hong Kong SAR, and the UK. We host events, workshops, exhibitions, and much more, helping children develop multiple skills, socialise, and generally have fun.
