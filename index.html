Who We Are

Education & Opportunity for Every Child

Founded in 2019, Ushaqlarimiza Öyrədək — meaning "Let's Teach Our Children" — is a non-governmental organisation rooted in the belief that no child's future should be determined by the circumstances of their birth.

We work directly with underprivileged communities across Azerbaijan, providing educational support, organising enriching community events, and advocating for child welfare at every level. Our volunteers and staff show up every single day because they believe in the transformative power of education.

From tutoring sessions to cultural festivals, from safe-space initiatives to scholarship programmes — everything we do is designed to unlock each child's unique potential and show them that the world is full of possibility.
